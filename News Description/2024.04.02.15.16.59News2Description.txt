Cognizant has once again been recognized as one of “America's Most Innovative Companies” by Fortune Magazine. To determine the top 200 companies, Fortune and Statista, the world-leading statistics portal and industry ranking provider, conducted a series of online surveys with both employees and industry experts to evaluate product innovation, process innovation and innovation culture.  It’s an honor to be recognized by Fortune as one of America’s top 200 Most Innovative Companies and a testament to our associates. and decades-long focus on innovation.
